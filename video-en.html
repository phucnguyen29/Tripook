Hung Kings Temple in Can Tho

A sacred destination preserving the soul of the nation in the heart of the Mekong Delta

Imagine yourself walking through a solemn space, standing before the majestic statue that symbolizes the roots of the Vietnamese people, where generations pay tribute to the Hung Kings for founding the nation. Located in the heart of Can Tho, the Hung Kings Temple is not only a unique architectural landmark but also a sacred symbol of national unity, preserving the timeless tradition of “drinking water, remember its source.”

At the Hung Kings Temple, you will experience the harmony between modern beauty and the profound depth of Vietnamese culture. It is a meaningful destination for worship, reflection, and remembrance, where every visitor can ignite their national pride and reconnect with the origins of Vietnam.
